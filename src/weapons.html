Weapons

Classic

The Classic is a pistol that all players receive upon spawning. It has two firing modes, a standard and semi-automatic single-bullet primary fire and a semi-automatic shotgun alternate fire that fires three bullets.

Cost: Free
Shorty

The Shorty is the cheapeast purchaseable weapon in the game. it is a semi-automatic shotgun with a low wall penetration

Cost: $300
Frenzy

The Frenzy is a fully automatic pistol with a low cost and capacity. It's most effective at close range, where you can quickly spray down the enemy.

Cost: $450
Ghost

The Ghost is a low-cost semi-automatic pistol. Equipped witha large magazine and a silencer, it can be used to spam through vision blockers without much risk to the players

Cost: $500
Sheriff

The Sheriff is a semi-automatic revolver and the most expensive sidearm in the game. Able to kill enemies witha single bullet to the head at most ranges and health totals.

Cost: $800
Bucky

The Bucky is one of the two shotguns available in Valorant. The Bucky holds 5 rounds and is an extremely effective weapon at close range.

Cost: $850
Judge

The Judge is the most expensive shotgun in Valorant, It is fully automatic shotgun with a medium wall penetraion. In very close range and with the right aim, you can eliminate the opponent with just one shot

Cost: $1850
Stinger

The Stinger is a low-cost SMG. It has two firing modes, a standard automatic single-bullet primary fire and a semi-automatic burst-mode that fires four bullets in a burst.

Cost: $1100
Spectre

The Spectre is a moderately-priced SMG equipped with a large amgazine and a ilencer, it can be used to spam through vision blockers without much risk to the player.

Cost: $1600
Bulldog

The Bulldog is a moderately-priced rifle. It has two firing moes, a standard automatic single-bullet primary fire and a semi-automatic burst=mode alternate fire.

Cost: $2050
Guardian

The Guardian is a high penetration slow-firing rifle that deals an insane amount of damage. It can one-tap headshot at any range, and can also go through some walls.

Cost: $2250
Phantom

The Phantom is a high-cost automatic rifle. One of Valorants best general-purpose weapons, it is most effective at short to medium range where it is able to spray down multiple enemies at once due to its high fire rate.

Cost: $2900
Vandal

The Vandal is a high-cost automatic rifle. One of Valorants best general-purpose weapons, it is most effective at picking off individual targers at longer ranges due to having no falloff, allowing it to kill any enemy with a single headshot at all ranges.

Cost: $2900
Marshal

The Marshal is a low cost sniper rifle. As a sniper, it has a scope with a single zoom setting on its Alternate Fire mode that has perfect accuracy when standing still.

Cost: $950
Outlaw

The Outlaw is a sniper rifle. The weapons has a high wall penetration and deals significant damage in even body shots. However it only contains two bullets within a magazine

Cost: $2400
Operator

The Operator is a high-cost sniper rifle and the most expensive gun in the game. As a sniper, it has a scope with two zoom settings on its Alternate Fire mode that has perfect accuracy when standing still.

Cost: $4700
Ares

The Ares is a medium-cost machine gun. As a machine gun, it is most effective as either a defensive tool to hold a site or when used to get wallbang kills due to its high penetration and rate of fire.

Cost: $1600
Odin

The Odin is a high-cost machine gun. When using primary fire, the odin's fire rate will increase over the duration the weapon is fired to a maximum amount, but using Alternate fire will allow the user to shoot at the maximum fire rate immediately

Cost: $3200
