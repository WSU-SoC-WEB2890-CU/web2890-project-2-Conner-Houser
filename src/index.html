What is Valorant?

Valorant is a popular tactical first-person shooter (FPS) game developed and published by Riot Games. The game combines elements of traditional FPS gameplay with unique character abilities and team-based strategy, like games like Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) and Overwatch. Players are divided into two teams, attacking and defending, with each match typically consisting of several rounds.
